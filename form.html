What we are looking for:

We are creating a walking tour for the Bronzeville neighborhood. We want to hear from community members about what stops and points of interest should be included. In other words, if you were giving someone a tour of the neighborhood where would you take them and what stories would you tell? They can be about the past, present, and future. Feel free to submit more than one story.
